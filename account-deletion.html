Account Deletion

Last Updated: November 2025

This action is permanent

Deleting your account will erase your personal data, transaction history, and preferences from our servers. This action cannot be undone. Once deleted, you will not be able to recover your account or retrieve any information.

How to Request Deletion

To ensure the security of your data and verify your identity, we process deletion requests manually via email. Please send a request from your registered email address.

Email To:

support@madhavfinance.com

Subject Line:

Request for Account Deletion

Draft Email Request

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?

We typically process deletion requests within 5-7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

What data is retained?

We may retain certain data as required by law for financial record-keeping and audit purposes, even after your account is closed.
